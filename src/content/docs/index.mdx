## About K5M

K5M is focused on building efficient, local-first applications and tools. Our projects prioritize:

- **Privacy**: Your data stays on your device
- **Performance**: Fast, lightweight applications
- **Simplicity**: Easy to use, easy to understand
- **Open Source**: Transparent and community-driven
